# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers and platform operators monitoring workspace-scoped AI gateway traffic. They use the console to diagnose routing, caching, usage, cost, and request behavior without leaving the operational flow.

## Product Purpose

Provide clear evidence about how logical models resolve to providers, what traffic costs, and where operational behavior needs attention. Success means an operator can understand current state and act on a problem quickly.

## Brand Personality

Dense, quiet, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Marketing-dashboard theatrics, decorative cards, ambiguous metrics, overlapping data labels, provider-specific widgets presented as universal insights, and visual novelty that slows scanning.

## Design Principles

- Make every panel answer a concrete operational question.
- Keep provider-neutral workflows neutral; disclose provider-specific behavior explicitly.
- Prefer familiar, compact controls and layouts over invented affordances.
- Preserve context while making anomalies and actions easy to find.
- Remove information that does not help an operator decide or diagnose.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard access, visible focus states, readable contrast, non-color status cues, and reduced-motion behavior across both themes.
